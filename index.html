Welcome to Anonim Community

This website is the official place for everything related to the Anonim Community project. You can find detailed information about the project, news, and all necessary resources in the Settings ⚙️

The project consists of the following modules:

  • AnonimCommunity: Our main module, founded in 2023.
  • AnonimForum: A secondary module founded in 2024, under further development.
  • AnonimWiki: A module in progress.
  • AnonimWorld: Planned for the future.
